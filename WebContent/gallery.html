Our industrial infrastructure

Reliable and durable pipe infrastructure solutions are required to eliminate losses, save energy and better serve communities and industries in the future. As a non-toxic material, Polyvinyl Chloride (PVC) infrastructure piping has been extensively tested and used for decades by numerous government agencies and is now replacing most of the previously used piping materials. It is not a major source of dioxin, does not collect bacteria, and has been found to have environmental benefit equal or better than other competing infrastructure piping materials like cast iron or concrete.
